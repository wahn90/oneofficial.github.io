NOTEBOOKIE-MOCKUPS PACK

Notebookie by Bulbfish is 12 craft notepad mockups for Adobe Photoshop. New, old, torn, closed, opened, etc. – everything is here. Every piece of this project was made from scratch and carefully assembled into detailed, high-res and useful mockups with a fancy authentic vibe.

FEATURES

  • Easy to use /> Three types of notebooks: new, used, damaged
  • Easy to use /> You can place your artwork on the notepad cover as well as on the notepad sheet
  • Easy to use /> Enough pixels for any user case
  • Easy to use / Well organized layers, Smart Objects, instructions included
  • Works on any background / Images you create will look nice on any background color. Shadow layer is optional and customizable: you can adjust shadow color and density or turn it off completely
  • Bonus 1 / Extra paper piece with the clip mockup included. You can use it separately or add as a final touch to the notebookie design
  • Bonus 2 / Isolated customizable coffee stains (in case you think these notebookies haven’t suffered enough)
  • Top quality / High resolution and sharp images, carefully organized layers, made by Bulbfish Design team with love and passion
